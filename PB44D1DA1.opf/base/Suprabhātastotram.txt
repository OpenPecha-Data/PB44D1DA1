dviradadaśanapāṇḍuḥ śītaraśmiḥ śaśāṅka-stilaka iva rajanyāḥ sarvacūḍāmaṇiryaḥ |avigatamadarāgaḥ
sarvathā so'pi suptodaśabala tava nityaṃ suprabhātaṃ prabhātam

prabalabhujacatuṣkaḥ ṣoḍaśārdhārdhavaktrojapaniyamavidhijñaḥ sāmavedapravaktā |amalakamalayoniḥ

himagiriśikharasthaḥ sarpayajñopavītītripuradahanadakṣo vyāghracarmottarīyaḥ |saha girivaraputryā

jvalitakuliśapāṇirdurjayo dānavānāṃsurapatirapi śacyā vibhrame mūḍhacetāḥ |aniśiniśiprasuptaḥ

kapilajaṭakalāpo raktatāmrāruṇākṣaḥpaśupatiratikāle saṅgabhaṅgaikadakṣaḥ |smaraśaradalitāṅgaḥ

vāruṇīpānamattodaśabala tava nityaṃ suprabhātaṃ prabhātam

atasikusumanīlo yasya śaktiḥ karāgrenavakamalavapuṣmān ṣaṇmukhaḥ krauñcahantā |trinayanatanayo'sau
